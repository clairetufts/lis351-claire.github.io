Going to concerts is one of my favorite things to do in the summer. I have seen a handful of different artists with different genres of music. I've seen rock artists, rap artists, pop artists, and country artists. I think my favorite is country concerts because the vibes and the ambiance is really cool. My top 3 favorite concerts I've been to are:

  • Motley Crue and Def Leopard
  • Zach Bryan
  • Metallica

My family and I went to the Motley Crue and Def Leopard concert 2 summers ago at US bank stadium. The history of Motley Crue is interesting to me and more information can be found in this website:

Motley Crue History

Last summer I went to the Zach Bryan concert with my sister and some of our friends. He is one of my favorite artists so it was really fun seeing him in concert.

I went to the Metallica concert with my family last summer. It was a great night and I had a lot of fun!
