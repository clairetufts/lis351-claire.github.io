Going on vacations are what I look foreward too every year and I've been to some pretty cool places. Here are a few of my favorite places I have travelled to.

My family and I went to Cabo over New Year's this year. We went snorkling, saw whales, swam in the ocean, and a lot more. It was a great trip!

A few years ago I went to Hawaii with my family. We stayed on Maui and it was my most favorite vacation I've been on. It is so pretty there and my favorite part was going on a helicopter ride.

My friends and I went to Cancun for our senior spring break. We went for a week and had a great time. We ate a lot of good food and had so much fun!
