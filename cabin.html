My cabin is my favorit place on Earth. It is located in Turtle Lake Wisconsin, and is about a 2 hour drive from my house. The cabin is on small lake called Pipe Lake and my favorite thing to do there are going on boat rides, jetskiing, and tanning with my sister. We also do a lot of fishing and make campfires every night.

This is a picture of my friends and I swimming in the lake. I had 7 of my bestfriends come up to my cabin last summer for a weekend and we had a great time!

This is my grandpa and I on the 4th of July. Every year he puts on a big fireworks show for the whole lake and everyone looks foreward to it every year.

Here is a picture of my cousins and I on the 4th of July. We always have the whole family at the cabin every 4th of July and we play games and eat good food.
